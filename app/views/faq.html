The Oracle of Doom

  • play epic Heavy Metal
  • draw inspiration from mainly European Ancient and Mediaeval history, fantasy literature and Oneiric experiences.
  • initially derived their pseudonyms from the names carved on tombstones on the cover of Warlord's Deliver Us EP.
  • do not glorify war. On the contrary, emphasise the tragic aspects of it.
  • hail from Lombardy. “Lombardy” comes from the term Langbard, an ancient Germanic tribe which migrated from Southern Scandinavia to Northern Italy. Langbardr is one of the names of Odin meaning Long Beard.

    As Lombards, have Celtic, Etruscan, Roman, Goth, Germanic, Byzantine, Frankish, Hohenstaufen, Habshurg, Austrian and - naturally - Italian heritage.
